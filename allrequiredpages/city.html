Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How much does a dumpster rental cost near me?

A: Prices vary by size, location, and debris type but ADJ Dumpster Rental offers transparent, all-inclusive rates. A 10-yard starts at ~$349, 20-yard at ~$449, 30-yard at ~$549, and 40-yard at ~$649 (regional adjustments apply). No fuel surcharges, no admin fees. Get your exact local dumpster rental prices with a 60-second quote.

Q2: How long can I keep a rented dumpster?

A: Standard rental periods are 7–10 days, but we offer flexible options from 1-day same-day hauls to 30-day extended stays (small fee applies after day 10). Just let us know your timeline upfront.

Q3: What items are NOT allowed in roll-off dumpsters?

A: Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, asbestos), tires, batteries, propane tanks, and appliances with Freon are prohibited nationwide due to landfill regulations. We provide a clear “Do/Don’t” guide with every rental.

Q4: Do I need a permit for a dumpster on my property?

A: If placing on private property (driveway, yard), usually *no*. If blocking a sidewalk, street, or public right-of-way, most cities require a permit which we can help you secure in under 24 hours in most markets.

Q5: How far in advance should I book a dumpster?

A: For standard jobs, 1–2 days is ideal. For weekends or high-demand seasons (spring/fall), 3–4 days ensures availability. But for same-day emergency needs, call by 10 AM, we’ll do our best.
